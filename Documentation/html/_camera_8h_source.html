21 * The Camera class encapsulates the properties and behaviors of a virtual camera

24 * orientation, and projection parameters, and provides methods to manipulate
25 * its transform and retrieve view/projection matrices.
26 *
27 * Key features:
28 * - Switchable between perspective and orthographic projections
29 * - Position and rotation management using Vector3 and Quaternion
30 * - View and projection matrix generation
31 * - Utility methods for moving, rotating, and looking at targets
32 *
33 * @note The camera uses a right-handed coordinate system.
34 * @note The up vector is assumed to be (0, 1, 0) unless specified otherwise.
35 *
36 * @example
37 * @code
38 * Camera camera(CameraType::Perspective, 45.0f, 16.0f/9.0f, 0.1f, 100.0f);
